What is anetwork marketing

Network marketing (or multi-level marketing; English multilevel marketing, MLM) - the concept of selling goods and services based on creating a network of independent distributors (sales agents), each of which, in addition to marketing products, also has the right to attract partners with similar rights . At the same time, the income of each member of the network consists of commissions for the sale of products and additional remuneration (bonuses), depending on the volume of sales made by the sales agents attracted by them.
